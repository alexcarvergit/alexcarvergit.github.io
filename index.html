About me

Hey there! My name's Alex and I'm a 17 year old coder from London. Currently studying Computing at NESCOT college in Surrey, and coding things in my spare time (including this website!). I was first introduced into the world of coding in 2011 by my good mate Tommy, who at the time was developing iPhone apps. I was fascinated by the endless possibilities of what you could create, so shortly after I looked up how to make some of my own apps. Unfortunately I didn't have a Mac at the time, so I instead began to look at HTML. Through my own dedication, YouTube and the help of coding clubs, I am where I am at today.
Currently I am experienced at:
  • HTML/CSS
  • JavaScript
  • C
  • C#
  • Python
  • Binge watching Netflix
However I am not afraid to challenge myself with something new.
